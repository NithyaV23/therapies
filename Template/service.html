Individual Therapy

Life has its seasons — some light, others heavy. If you’re carrying anxiety, grief, exhaustion, or self-doubt, these sessions are a place to set it down for a while. We’ll bring in tools like CBT and mindfulness, but more than that, we’ll make room for you to feel steady again.

More Info

Couples Therapy

Every relationship has its moments — of closeness, of distance, of trying to understand one another. Couples therapy is about having a space to talk through conflicts, communication struggles, or trust concerns with guidance and care, so you can find your way back to each other.

More Info

Family Therapy

Families are beautifully complex. Sometimes they lift us up, sometimes they leave us tangled. Family sessions create space to untangle those knots — working through conflict, stress, or communication breakdowns — and finding healthier ways to connect.

More Info

Pre-Marital Counselling

Preparing for marriage is exciting, but also full of questions. These sessions explore expectations, communication, and differences so that you step into this new chapter with clarity, understanding, and a strong foundation.
