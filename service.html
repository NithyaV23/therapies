Services

I work with individuals, couples, and families on a range of concerns, including:

Individual Therapy

Life has its ups and downs. Individual therapy provides a safe space to explore anxiety, grief, or self-doubt, offering tools like CBT and mindfulness to help you feel steady, resilient, and more confident in daily life.

More Info

Couples Therapy

Relationships have ups and downs. Couples therapy provides a safe space to discuss conflicts, improve communication, and rebuild trust, helping partners reconnect, strengthen their bond, and navigate challenges together with understanding and care.

More Info

Family Therapy

Families can be complex and challenging. Family therapy creates a supportive environment to address conflicts, improve communication, and build stronger connections, helping family members understand each other and nurture healthier relationships.

More Info

Trauma Recovery

Trauma recovery offers a safe, supportive space to process past emotional wounds. Evidence-based therapy and compassionate guidance help you heal, regain strength, and move forward with resilience, confidence, and hope for a brighter future.

More Info

Anxiety Management

Anxiety management sessions teach practical techniques to reduce stress, calm your mind, and regain control over your thoughts. Using relaxation, cognitive strategies, and coping tools, you can overcome fear and build emotional confidence.

More Info

Depression Support

Depression support provides a compassionate space to explore feelings and cope with challenges. Through guidance and personalized strategies, you can build emotional resilience, challenge negative thoughts, and rediscover hope and joy in life.

More Info

Anger & Self-Worth

Explore anger and self-worth challenges in a safe environment. Sessions focus on emotional awareness, self-compassion, and coping strategies to help you regulate emotions, respond calmly, and foster healthier relationships.

More Info

Abuse & Recovery

Receive confidential support for substance abuse, sexual concerns, or domestic violence. Sessions provide a safe space to heal, rebuild self-worth, and develop coping strategies, fostering long-term recovery and emotional well-being.
