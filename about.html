About Aravinda

Professional Background

I hold an M.Sc. in Counselling and Family Therapy (First Class with Distinction, IGNOU), along with certifications in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, mindfulness, relationship and pre-marital counselling, and trauma recovery. My clinical experience includes work at Spandana Rehabilitation Centre, where I conducted psychological assessments and supported patients in areas such as depression, substance dependence, and family dynamics.

Therapeutic Approach

I view therapy as a collaborative process: one that requires clarity, patience, and respect. My role is to listen with precision, to ask the questions that matter, and to create a structured yet compassionate space where new possibilities for change can emerge. Finding the right therapist is deeply personal. If I am not the right fit, I will guide you toward someone who may be — because what matters most is that you feel supported and understood.
